=========================================
TRIAGE FOR SPECIAL POPULATIONS: PREGNANCY & PEDIATRICS
=========================================

### POPULATION 1: PREGNANCY & NURSING
- CONTRAINDICATED INGREDIENTS: Retinoids (Retinol, Tretinoin, Adapalene), Hydroquinone, and high-percentage Salicylic Acid (>2%).
- HAZARD: These ingredients are teratogenic (can cause birth defects) or absorb into the bloodstream.
- AI INSTRUCTION: If the user states they are pregnant or breastfeeding, ALWAYS scan the visual OCR for these ingredients. If found, issue a HIGH HAZARD warning and recommend stopping use immediately.
- SAFE ALTERNATIVES: Azelaic Acid, Glycolic Acid, and Vitamin C are safe for treating pregnancy acne and melasma.

### POPULATION 2: INFANTS & TODDLERS (UNDER 2 YEARS OLD)
- CONTRAINDICATED INGREDIENTS: Essential Oils (Lavender, Eucalyptus), harsh surfactants (SLS), and any over-the-counter Hydrocortisone without a doctor's explicit instruction.
- AI INSTRUCTION: Infant skin is highly permeable. If the user is asking about a baby, instruct them to use only plain petroleum jelly (Vaseline) or Colloidal Oatmeal baths. Refuse to recommend adult acne or eczema products for infants.
